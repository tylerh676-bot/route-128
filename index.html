Route 128

Route 128 is a wild area in the Hoenn region, featured in the third generation of Pokémon games. It is best known for the underwater entrance to the mysterious Seafloor Cavern, where the climactic events involving Team Aqua, Team Magma, Kyogre, and Groudon unfold in the Hoenn games. Trainers must use both Surf and Dive to fully explore the route, making it one of the most important ocean areas in the region.

Team Aqua/Magma

As the player travels through Route 128, they discover that Team Aqua or Team Magma has located the hidden entrance to the Seafloor Cavern beneath the ocean. The villainous team believes that awakening a legendary Pokémon will help them reshape the world—Team Aqua seeks to expand the seas with Kyogre, while Team Magma hopes to increase the landmass with Groudon.

After diving underwater and finding the cavern, the player battles through the team's members and confronts their leader. Although the leader succeeds in awakening the legendary Pokémon, they soon realize they have lost control of its immense power. This mistake sets off a crisis across Hoenn and leads to the dramatic events that unfold in Sootopolis City.
